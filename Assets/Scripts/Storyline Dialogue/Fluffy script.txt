Fluffy’s Goals


Fluffy wants to trick the player
Fluffy wants to destroy the player
Fluffy wants to torture the player
Fluffy is driven by sadistic glee 


Fluffy’s Backstory


Fluffy was once a nice friendly animal but upon learning the dark arts, he found himself experiencing the overwhelming weight of the entire knowledge of the cosmos. As a result of his studies, Fluffy’s personality changed. He had seen the nature of God and the Devil and Fluffy lost hope in reality. Now, he finds others as tools and toys to secrete their suffering in order to stay young and fluffy forever. The player is just another victim, found from another dimension. Fluffy requires the victim to willfully enter the top of his wizard tower to become Fluffy’s torture slave.
